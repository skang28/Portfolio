Some of my Projects

Just a Calendar


Just a Calendar's name speaks for itself. It's a fresh and clean take on the classic calendar app that almost everyone uses on a daily basis. This app focuses on the essentials, and strips away the rest! This app is for users who like to keep things simple. Users can still access a calendar in multiple views, and they can create events so that they'll never lose track of what's coming up next!

Technology used: HTML, CSS, Javascript, React, Node.js, Express, PostgreSQL

Honest Takes


Honest Takes is an online forum for open and anonymous discussion centered around sports! This app is for users who enjoy the idea of anonymity, but still want to talk to others. Every day a new topic about what's buzzing in sports will appear, and users create their own posts to respond to the topic or comment on other people's posts.

Technology used: HTML, CSS, Javascript, React, Node.js, Express, PostgreSQL

What to Watch


This app is for the user looking for something to watch, but just can't choose. Users can select the category movie or TV show and the app will give you a random selection to watch! It even gives you an iMDb rating, and links to iMDb and Rotten Tomatoes for easy access to reviews.

Technology used: HTML, CSS, Javascript, jQuery
